About

Whoami

I'm Dominik Tropp, a self-taught developer — no degree, no bootcamp, just a lot of broken builds and late-night documentation deep-dives.

I'm comfortable jumping between scripting languages depending on what a problem actually needs, rather than sticking to one stack out of habit. If something needs to be automated, scraped, glued together, or shipped fast, I'll find the right tool and figure out the rest along the way.

Outside of shipping code, I moderate and co-own several Discord communities — keeping things running, the bots working, and the people in them civil.
